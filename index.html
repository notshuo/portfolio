Currently in my fourth year at Don Honorio Ventura State University, I am focused on expanding my skills in graphic design, web development, and computer networking. I thrive in hands-on projects and enjoy applying my knowledge to real-world challenges. Experience

Additionally, I designed a simple e-commerce cake website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of another school project.
